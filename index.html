The Mirthmancer

Bard. Philocalist.
Truth-seeker.

Welcome to a space suspended between the playful and the profound, where a well-placed word may unravel something you didn't know was tangled within you. I find myself constantly reaching deeper to discover the secrets hidden inside us.

My hope is that by entering my world and embracing my perspective, you learn something about your own existence.

My work spans poetry, lyrics, spoken word, and song. Whether I'm narrating a story, bringing a character to life, reading poetry, or performing original music, I bring warmth, curiosity, and intention with every breath.
